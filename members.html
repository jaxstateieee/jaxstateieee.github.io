Why Join IEEE?

Networking

Meet industry professionals, alumni, professors and fellow students.

Projects & Competitions

Work on real hardware/software projects and compete regionally/nationally.

Career Boost

IEEE membership looks excellent on resumes and LinkedIn.

How to Join

  1. Go to ieee.org/membership
  2. Select Student Membership (~$32/year)
  3. Choose Jacksonville State University as your school
  4. Join the IEEE JSU Student Branch during registration or email us afterward
