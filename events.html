Jacksonville State University Students Excel at IEEE SoutheastCon 2025 Under Leadership of Dr. Arup Ghosh

Charlotte, NC

A group of ten Jacksonville State University computer science students, guided by their professor Dr. Arup Ghosh, traveled to Charlotte, North Carolina to compete against some of the best engineering students in the Southeast at a major IEEE conference. They took on challenges in everything from circuit design to software building, and walked away with a third-place finish in the software competition — where they had to design and build a working project under serious time pressure. On top of that, two of the students presented their own published research, one on keeping teenagers safe online and another on using AI to help care for elderly people. It was a big moment for JSU, showing that their students can hold their own against top programs from across the region.
