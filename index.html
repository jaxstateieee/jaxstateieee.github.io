WHO WE ARE

IEEE JSU Student Branch

We connect Gamecock engineers to the world's largest technical professional community. Through hands-on projects, cutting-edge workshops, regional competitions, and industry networking, we turn classroom knowledge into real-world impact.

As an active member of IEEE Region 3 (Southeast US), we proudly compete at SoutheastCon every year - specializing in embedded systems, robotics, PCB design, Web Development, and software engineering - building skills that employers actively seek.
