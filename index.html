WHO WE ARE

IEEE JSU

The IEEE Student Branch at Jacksonville State University connects students with the world's largest technical professional organization. We bridge classroom theory and real-world engineering through hands-on projects, workshops, and competitions.

Our branch is part of IEEE Region 3 (Southeastern US) and sends teams to SoutheastCon annually. We focus on embedded systems, robotics, PCB design, and software development — giving members practical skills that employers value.
